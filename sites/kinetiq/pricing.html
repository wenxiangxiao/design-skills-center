We never stop tracing mid-month. You get a 20% overage grace at no charge; beyond that we switch to statistical sampling and email you before anything changes. Upgrade whenever — quota changes apply instantly, prorated.

Full Momentum, no card. When the trial ends you drop to Trace automatically — nothing is deleted for 30 days, so an upgrade restores your full history. No sales call unless you ask for one.
